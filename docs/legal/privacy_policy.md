This Privacy Policy explains how SightSinger.ai ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use SightSinger.ai ("Service"). We comply with the UK GDPR and the EU GDPR where applicable.

SightSinger.ai is the data controller for personal data processed through the Service.
